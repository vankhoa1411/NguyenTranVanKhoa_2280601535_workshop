### Overall Evaluation

**1. Learning and Working Environment**  
The First Cloud AI Journey provided a professional, friendly, and motivating learning environment. Everyone was approachable and willing to share knowledge whenever I encountered difficulties. The positive atmosphere made it easy for me to adapt and stay motivated throughout the program.

**2. Support from Mentors and Program Administrators**  
The mentors were highly supportive and always took the time to explain concepts clearly whenever I had questions. They encouraged independent learning by guiding me toward solutions instead of simply providing answers. The program administrators were also very responsive, ensuring that learning materials, announcements, and schedules were well organized and easy to access.

**3. Relevance of the Program to My Academic Background**  
The program content was highly relevant to my Computer Science studies, particularly in the field of Cloud Computing. It effectively bridged the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications through hands-on labs and practical exercises.

**4. Learning Opportunities and Skill Development**  
Throughout the program, I gained valuable experience working with various AWS services while improving my technical, problem-solving, teamwork, and time management skills. I also developed the habit of independently researching documentation and troubleshooting technical issues, which are essential skills for a Cloud Engineer.

**5. Culture and Team Collaboration**  
The FCAJ community fostered a collaborative and supportive culture. Participants were always willing to exchange ideas, discuss technical topics, and help one another complete assignments and labs. This collaborative environment made learning both enjoyable and productive.

**6. Training Program and Learner Support**  
The program followed a well-structured learning roadmap, progressing from fundamental concepts to more advanced topics. The combination of theoretical lessons, practical labs, and mentoring sessions significantly enhanced my understanding of AWS Cloud services and their real-world applications.

### Additional Questions

- **What was the most satisfying part of your experience in the program?**  
The most rewarding aspect was having the opportunity to learn directly from experienced mentors while gaining hands-on experience with AWS services. The practical labs helped me build confidence in deploying and managing cloud resources, and the program gave me a much clearer understanding of Cloud Computing as a career path.

- **What do you think could be improved for future participants?**  
I believe the program could include more technical workshops, guest speaker sessions with Cloud professionals, and additional advanced labs or mini-projects. These activities would allow participants to gain even more practical experience and exposure to real-world scenarios.

- **Would you recommend this program to your friends? Why?**  
Yes, absolutely. I would highly recommend the First Cloud AI Journey to anyone interested in Cloud Computing. The program offers a clear learning roadmap, dedicated mentors, high-quality learning materials, and valuable hands-on experience with AWS, making it an excellent opportunity for students to prepare for future careers.

### Suggestions & Future Expectations

- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the program experience?**  
I would love to see more team-based projects, career orientation sessions, and real-world case studies. These additions would help participants better understand how cloud technologies are applied in enterprise environments.

- **Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?**  
Yes. Given the opportunity, I would be delighted to join more advanced FCAJ programs or even contribute as a mentor in the future, sharing my knowledge and experiences with new participants.

- **Additional Comments (Optional):**  
I would like to express my sincere appreciation to the entire First Cloud AI Journey team, including the mentors and program administrators, for their continuous guidance and support throughout the program. This experience has significantly improved both my technical knowledge and professional skills. I hope FCAJ continues to grow and provide more students with valuable opportunities to explore Cloud Computing and AWS.
